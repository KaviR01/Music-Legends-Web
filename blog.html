Albums

BTS, also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a globally renowned South Korean boy band that has made a significant impact on the music industry. Since their debut in 2013, BTS has released a diverse and extensive discography that has garnered a massive fanbase worldwide, known as the "ARMY." Their music combines various genres, including K-pop, hip-hop, R&B, and pop, and their lyrics often explore themes such as youth, self-identity, love,and personal growth.


BTS's discography includes several albums and singles, and some of their most notable works include:


  • "2 Cool 4 Skool" (2013) - Their debut single album that introduced them to the music scene.
  • "Wings" (2016) - An album that catapulted them to international fame, featuring hits like "Blood Sweat & Tears" and "Spring Day"
  • "Love Yourself" series (2017-2018) - A trilogy consisting of "Love Yourself: Her," "Love Yourself: Tear," and "Love Yourself: Answer." These albums included chart-toppers like "DNA," "Fake Love," and "IDOL"
  • "Map of the Soul" series (2019-2020) - Another trilogy with "Map of the Soul: Persona," "Map of the Soul: 7," and "BE." These albums explored themes of self-discovery and resilience and included hits like "Boy with Luv" and "Dynamite"

BTS has also released many Japanese albums and collaborations with artists from around the world. They are known for their high-energy performances, meaningful lyrics, and socially conscious music. Their influence extends beyond music, as they have become global ambassadors for South Korea and advocates for youth empowerment. Their discography continues to evolve and grow, making them one of the most iconic and successful music acts of the 21st century.

Acheivements

BTS, the South Korean boy band, has achieved remarkable success and numerous milestones from their debut in 2013 to 2023. Here is a summary of their achievements during this period:


2013-2014: Debut and Early Years


  • BTS made their debut on June 13, 2013, with the single album "2 Cool 4 Skool."
  • They earned several New Artist of the Year awards in South Korea
  • In 2014, they released their first mini-album, "Skool Luv Affair," which included hits like "Boy in Luv"

2015-2016: International Breakthrough


  • BTS began gaining international recognition with albums like "The Most Beautiful Moment in Life, Part 1" and "The Most Beautiful Moment in Life, Part 2."
  • They embarked on their first world tour, "The Red Bullet Tour."
  • Their single "Dope" became a major hit in South Korea and beyond

2017-2018: Love Yourself Trilogy


  • BTS released the "Love Yourself" trilogy, with each album achieving massive success and topping charts worldwide.
  • The music video for "DNA" reached over 1 billion views on YouTube.
  • They became the first K-pop group to perform at the American Music Awards
  • BTS made their debut on the Billboard Hot 100 with "MIC Drop" and "Fake Love."
  • They addressed the United Nations General Assembly and launched the "Love Myself" campaign in partnership with UNICEF.

2019-2020: Map of the Soul Era


  • BTS released the "Map of the Soul" series, with "Map of the Soul: Persona" becoming their third Billboard 200 chart-topper.
  • They became the first South Korean act to perform at Wembley Stadium in London.
  • The group earned their first Grammy nomination for "Best Recording Package."
  • Their single "Dynamite" became their first No. 1 hit on the Billboard Hot 100.
  • BTS delivered a virtual commencement speech for the Class of 2020 at the "Dear Class of 2020" event.

2021-2023: Further Success and Global Impact


  • BTS continued to dominate the Billboard charts with the release of "BE."
  • They achieved numerous chart-topping singles, including "Butter," "Permission to Dance," and "My Universe" (a collaboration with Coldplay)
  • BTS performed at the 2021 Grammy Awards.
  • They continued to break records on social media, YouTube, and streaming platforms
  • BTS's influence expanded to various industries, from fashion to philanthropy.
  • Their global fanbase, known as the "ARMY," continued to grow and support their endeavors

The BTS fanbase, known as the "ARMY," is one of the most passionate and dedicated in the world.


BTS's achievements go beyond just their music. They have made a significant impact on culture, youth empowerment, and global awareness of Korean culture. Their journey from a relatively unknown group to a global phenomenon is a testament to their talent, hard work, and the power of their message.They have broken numerous records, expanded the reach of K-pop, and contributed to the globalization of Korean culture.
